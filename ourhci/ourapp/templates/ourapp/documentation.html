Register


Before you can start using OurHCI, you will need an account. You can register for an account using the red "Register" button at the homepage.
You will be redirected to a form, prompting you for your name, class, email address, and the password you intend to use.
After you enter these information and press "Submit", your account will be created.


User Search


You can search for users by clicking on the " Users" tab on the navigation bar. This will redirect you to a search bar.
You can select to filter by name or by class with a dropdown, and enter your search query.
After pressing "Go", you will be directed to a user's page if there is a direct match, otherwise you will be directed to a list of users that match the query.


View Profiles


To view your own profile, you can click on the " Profile" button at the bottom of the navigation bar.
To view other users' profiles, you can search for them using the user search function, then click on their name. This will redirect you to their profile page.


Edit Profile


You can edit your profile by pressing the "" button on your profile. This will redirect you to a form.
This will enable you to edit your email address and biography (bio for short). To edit your username, you will need to contact an admin.


Following Users


You can follow users (non-mutual relationship) in OurHCI. This will enable you to see their posts when you go to the " Following" tab.


Creating Posts


To create a post, you can click on the " New" button on the navigation bar to the left. You will be taken to a form to create your post.
You can type in normal text, HTML, or Markdown.
Once you have composed the post, press:

  1. the button to send
    2. or
  2. Ctrl+S
to send the post. Please note that if your post contains swear words, it will not pass the check and your post will be deleted.


Editing Posts


To edit your post, press the "" button visible on the post you wish to edit. You will be taken to a form to edit your post.
Similarly, you can type in normal text, HTML, or Markdown.
Once you have complete your edits, press

  1. the button to send
    2. or
  2. Ctrl+S
to send the post. We would like to reiterate not to add any swear words to your posts during edits, as they will not pass the checks.


Deleting Posts


To delete your post, press the "" button visible on the post you wish to delete. A confirmation will pop up to ensure that you do not delete a post by accident.
After you delete your post, you will be redirected to the homepage and your post will be gone.


Viewing Posts


Posts can be viewed in two places:

  1. the " Home" tab, under "Posts"
    2. and
  2. the " Following" filter button on the home page, which shows posts by only people you follow.


Markdown


Markdown is one of the ways you can stylise text in your posts, the other method being HTML.
Some basic ways to format your text are headings (#), italic/bold(*, **), and lists ("1. 2. 3.", "- - -").
If you wish to use Markdown in your posts, this is a useful resource.
